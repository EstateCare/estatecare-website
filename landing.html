How It Works

Getting started is easy. We handle everything from the first walkthrough to your final inspection before you return.

01
Free Walk-Through
We visit your property, assess your needs, and recommend the right service plan before you commit to anything.
02
Service Begins
Your dedicated inspector is assigned based on proximity to your property. Regular inspections begin on your schedule.
03
You Stay Informed
After every inspection you receive a detailed photo report through your EstateCare dashboard. Issues flagged immediately.
04
Come Home Confident
A final inspection 2 days before your return ensures your home is ready. No surprises when you walk through the door.
